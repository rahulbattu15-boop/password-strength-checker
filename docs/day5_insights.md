\## Strength Distribution



\* Strong Passwords: 5,952

\## Character Analysis



\* 90.8% of passwords have letters.



\* 94.1% of passwords have characters.



\## Key Findings



1\. The majority of passwords in the dataset are strong.



2\. Longer passwords with a mix of characters make them stronger.



3\. Uppercase letters and special characters are often found in passwords.



\##Conclusion



The dataset shows that passwords, with characters and a mix of character types are usually stronger.
